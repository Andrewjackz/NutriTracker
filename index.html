📐 Exercise Burn Formula
Total Burn = Running Burn + Walking Burn

🏃 Running burn: kcal = 9.8 × weight_kg × (km × pace_min_per_km ÷ 60)
Based on MET = 9.8 for running. Running steps estimated at 900 steps/km.
Example: 5 km @ 5:30 pace, 85 kg → 9.8 × 85 × (5 × 5.5 ÷ 60) = 381 kcal

👟 Walking burn: kcal = (total steps − run steps) × 0.00057 × weight_kg
Based on MET = 3.8 for brisk walking (100 steps/min).
Example: 12,000 total steps, 4,500 run steps → 7,500 × 0.00057 × 85 = 363 kcal

Weight used: Same-day logged weight. Falls back to 80 kg if no weight recorded.

Note: These are estimates. Actual burn varies with fitness level, terrain, running efficiency and individual metabolism. Use as a directional guide, not a precise measurement.
