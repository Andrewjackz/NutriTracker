📐 Exercise Burn Formula
Formula: kcal = steps × 0.00057 × weight_kg
Derivation: Based on MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) for brisk walking (MET = 3.8).
MET formula: kcal = MET × weight_kg × duration_hours
Duration estimated as: steps ÷ 100 steps/min ÷ 60 min/hr
Simplified: kcal = 3.8 × weight_kg × (steps / 6000) = steps × 0.000633 × weight_kg
Adjusted constant 0.00057 accounts for partial overhead and average stride variation.

Example: 10,000 steps at 85 kg → 10,000 × 0.00057 × 85 = 485 kcal
Weight used: Same-day logged weight. Falls back to 80 kg if no weight recorded.

Note: This is an estimate. Actual burn varies with fitness level, terrain, pace and individual metabolism. Use as a directional guide, not a precise measurement.
